Likely

Facebook Page reaction invitation assistant

About

Likely processes visible Invite buttons inside a Facebook post reaction dialog that you deliberately open. You control the session limit, cooldown, pause, resume, and stop actions.

Privacy at a glance

Likely temporarily reads the open dialog's structure and button labels locally. It does not record or transmit names, profiles, reactions, credentials, browsing history, or other Facebook content. Your limit and cooldown settings are stored locally in Chrome.

Permissions

  • Active tab: temporary access to the Facebook tab you select.
  • Scripting: runs Likely's packaged page script in that tab.
  • Storage: remembers your controls locally.

Important

Likely cannot guarantee results or prevent platform restrictions. Stop if Facebook shows a warning, checkpoint, or unexpected result. Likely is not affiliated with Meta or Facebook.
